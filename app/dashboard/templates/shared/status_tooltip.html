Possible Statuses:
  1. Open: Bounty is funded, and available for a new developer to come tackle.
  2. Work Started Bounty is funded, Coding has been started by at least one developer.
  3. Work Submitted: Bounty is funded, Code has been completed, and bounty hunters have *submitted* a claim
  4. Work Done: The funder has accepted the work submission. The staked funds have been paid out.
Cancellation Statuses:
  1. Expired: Bounty was not completed by expiration date and has expired.
  2. Canceled: Bounty was not completed, and was canceled by the bounty submitter.
